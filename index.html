What the service does

Academic Plug organizes the parts students usually have to chase across five different tabs.

Use the site to move from subject pages to guided video review, quick questions, and then PlugGPT when you need an explanation that goes further.

Everything for free

No paywall between the student and the support.

Free access

Course pages, video review, practice questions, and PlugGPT routing are kept open for students.

No fragmented workflow

Students can revise, check understanding, and jump into AI support without leaving the platform flow.

Built around real school use

Designed for IB and high school work instead of generic productivity software.

We did our homework

The structure is based on the actual things students need during revision.

01 Courses first

Students should land in a clear subject path, not a random library of links.

02 Review with context

Video lessons, transcript summaries, and question sets reinforce the same topic from different angles.

03 PlugGPT when needed

When the standard materials are not enough, students can hand off directly into PlugGPT for guided help.

04 Focused on usefulness

The goal is not to look busy. The goal is to reduce friction and make study support faster to access.
